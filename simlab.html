Simulation Lab

Experiment with real‑world scenarios in a safe, interactive space. The Sim Lab uses games and simulations to teach budgeting, investing and decision‑making skills. Teachers and parents can play along and guide learners through the challenges.

Real Life Lab

In the Real Life Lab, students earn income, pay bills, choose careers and manage a credit score. Teachers act as bankers, offering loans or investment opportunities, and learners decide how to spend their weeks. This image shows one possible layout for a mobile version of the life simulation game.
