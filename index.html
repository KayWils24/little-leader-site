Our Mission

Little Leader’s mission is to empower African American children and families with the knowledge, skills and confidence to navigate their financial futures. Through culturally relevant games, simulations, lessons and collaborative activities aligned with Common Core and NGSS standards, we aim to make personal finance and STEM concepts accessible and engaging from kindergarten through high school. We partner with parents and educators to nurture curiosity, critical thinking and responsible decision‑making so every child can imagine, plan and achieve their boldest dreams.

Our Vision

We envision a generation of young leaders who see themselves as capable innovators and financial stewards. By providing real‑world experiences—managing budgets, investing, preparing for college and careers—in a supportive and joyful environment, Little Leader strives to close educational gaps and build enduring economic equity. Our long‑term goal is to inspire students to become confident problem‑solvers and compassionate community members who use their skills to uplift themselves and others.
