Parents & Teachers

Welcome! This portal provides guidance for adults who want to support children on their financial literacy journey. Use the resources below to reinforce what your learners experience in the Sim Lab and connect those lessons to everyday life.

How to Get Involved

  • Discuss budgeting together: Start by using the Budgeting Game to plan a monthly budget. Ask your child to think about needs versus wants and to set savings goals. Encourage them to reflect on their choices and how it feels to save for the future.
  • Explore investing and risk: Join your learner in the Stock and Real Estate simulations. Talk about how stock prices fluctuate and how owning property can build long‑term wealth. Discuss the importance of diversification and not putting all your money in one place.
  • Plan for college and careers: Use the College & Career Planner to compare tuition costs, scholarships, loans and career salaries. This exercise helps demystify the process of paying for higher education and weighing different career paths.
  • Watch lessons and complete quizzes: Watch the video lessons together and discuss key takeaways. The quizzes that follow each video reinforce comprehension and allow you to celebrate progress.
  • Offer encouragement: Positive affirmations can boost confidence. Celebrate badges and XP milestones and remind young learners that mistakes are part of the learning process.

Curriculum Alignment

The activities on Little Leader align with nationally recognised financial literacy standards, including earning income, spending, saving, investing, managing credit and managing risk【539083898243570†L386-L432】. They also incorporate mathematics concepts such as ratios, percentages and data analysis that are foundational in the Common Core State Standards.

Our science and engineering explorations connect finance to systems thinking and data modelling. This three‑dimensional learning reflects the Next Generation Science Standards’ emphasis on crosscutting concepts, scientific practices and core ideas【13214476530571†L100-L147】.
